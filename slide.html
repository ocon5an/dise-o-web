Stormveil Castle

Stormveil Castle is a Legacy Dungeon in Elden Ring. A castle that lies on the cliffs of Stormhill, a stronghold of Godrick the Grafted.

Raya Lucaria Academy

The Academy of Raya Lucaria is a Legacy Dungeon in Elden Ring. Quite a different area as it has a variety of blue hues, compared to the green open spaces of Limgrave.

Leyndell, Royal Capital

Leyndell, Royal Capital is a Legacy Dungeon in Elden Ring. A sprawling capital city that houses various strong enemies, as well as champions that were previously encountered.

Volcano Manor

Volcano Manor is a Legacy Dungeon in Elden Ring. Volcano Manor is unique in that much of its progression, if you so choose, can be done outside of the manor itself.
